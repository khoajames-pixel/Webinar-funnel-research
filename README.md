# Webinar Funnel from Zero – B2B SaaS Research

## Why This Topic

Webinars remain one of the most efficient levers for growth in B2B SaaS companies. They combine education, lead generation, and community-building all in one format. But most SaaS teams struggle to design a full funnel, from promotion to registration, engagement, and conversion after the event. In this project, I will analyze how the top experts in the world build webinar funnels from scratch.

## What This Repository Contains

- **[research/sources.md](./research/sources.md)** — A curated list of 10 experts and companies actively sharing webinar funnel strategies.
- **research/linkedin-posts/** — Collected LinkedIn posts from practitioners discussing webinar tactics.
- **research/youtube-transcripts/** — Transcripts from YouTube creators and SaaS marketers explaining webinar funnels.
- **research/other/** — Case studies, blog posts, and reports from SaaS platforms and agencies.
- **README.md** — This file, explaining the rationale, structure, and goals of the project.

## Why These 10 Experts

I optimized for **signal over volume** — 10 strong voices, not 50 generic ones. Selection rules:

- **Practitioners, not commentators** — every source actively runs webinars for themselves or paying B2B SaaS clients, with measurable results.
- **Full coverage of the discipline** — systematic funnel methodologies (Amy Porterfield), marketing and sales alignment strategies (Andrei Zinkevich), demand-gen strategists (Chris Walker, Dave Gerhardt, Daniel Murray, Udi Ledergor), SaaS operators turned educators (Ryan James, Sangram Vajre, Melissa Kwan), and GTM strategists (Mark Kilens).
- **Mixed source types** — LinkedIn posts, YouTube transcripts, and SaaS platform case studies.

Every expert was verified live (real profile, currently active) on **2026-06-12**.  
See **[research/sources.md](./research/sources.md)** for full rationale and links.
